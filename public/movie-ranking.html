The Best Movies According to Naveen

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Manichitrathazhu (1993)

A psychological thriller that intricately weaves elements of horror and suspense, leaving a lasting impact with its iconic performances and gripping storyline.

Drishyam (2013)

A riveting tale of a common man's extraordinary efforts to protect his family, blending suspense, drama, and ethical dilemmas into a cinematic masterpiece.

Premam (2015)

A coming-of-age romantic drama that beautifully captures the essence of love across different stages of life, resonating with audiences for its heartfelt portrayal of relationships.
